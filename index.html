Hello - I’m Yuanyuan Zhang 🔊

I am a 5th year Ph.D. candidate in Marketing at KU Leuven. I study questions at the intersection of marketing and public policy. Through my work, I aim to generate insights that inform managers in developing effective marketing strategies and policymakers in designing sound interventions.
